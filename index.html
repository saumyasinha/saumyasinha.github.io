Hello!

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Colorado, Boulder. I will join ISTerre Laboratory, Grenoble, France in October as an IRD (institut de recherche pour le développement) researcher!

Research Interests. My research focuses on applied maths and computer science, and more particularly machine learning techniques for computational sustainability, climate informatics and natural hazards. My previous research also included medical image analysis, machine learning for cardiac personalisation and inverse problems.

News! I am part of the 2019 Climate Informatics Workshop organizing committee that will be in Paris in October! All details on submission, travel grants and hackathon here. Link to last year's hackathon here.

Our 2019 PNTS grant with F. Karbou (MétéoFrance) is accepted! IRMAA: Indicateurs spatialisés de l'activité avalancheuse par imageRie sar sentinel-1 et Méthodes d'Apprentissage Automatique. poster link

Bio

  • 2018-2019. Post-doctoral position on climate machine learning at Colorado University, Boulder, USA (with Claire Monteleoni)
  • 2018. Post-doctoral CNRS position on climate informatics and storm predictions at LAL, Orsay Paris-Sud (with Claire Monteleoni, Balázs Kégl and Guillaume Charpiat)
  • 2014-2017. PhD candidate (PhD defense: Dec. 2017) on non-invasive personalisation of cardiac model at Inria Sophia-Antipolis (France) in the Asclepios/Epione team (under the supervision of M. Sermesant, H. Delingette and N. Ayache).
