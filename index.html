Hello!

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Colorado, Boulder. I am advised by Prof Claire Monteleoni.

Research Interests. I work on the intersection of computer vision and machine learning. I am particularly interested in developing methods that can enable robots to perform robustly alongside humans. I have applied machine learning techniques on satellite imagery for climate informatics.
