Hello!

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Colorado, Boulder. I work with Prof Claire Monteleoni in the Machine Learning lab at CU.

Research Interests. I work on the intersection of computer vision and machine learning with my research focused on building AI solutions to address urgent challenges related to climate change and sustainability. My interests broadly lie in developing unsupervised or semi-supervised learning algorithms, which are extremely useful in such applications.
