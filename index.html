Hello!

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Colorado, Boulder. I am advised by Prof Claire Monteleoni.

Research Interests. I work on the intersection of computer vision and machine learning. I am particularly interested in applying learning algorithms which use data as its own supervision in the field of robotics. I have applied machine learning techniques for climate informatics on satellite imagery.
